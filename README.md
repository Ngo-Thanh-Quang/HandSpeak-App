# HandSpeak - Sign Language App

Explore the world of American Sign Language comprehensively and interactively through an innovative cross-platform application built with Flutter and Python.

The application offers a rich learning experience, allowing users to access lessons by specific topics. After learning, students can reinforce their knowledge through multiple-choice quizzes, while teachers can easily create and customize quiz content for their students.

A unique feature of the application is the "Study Set," where users practice signs via video and are graded directly by artificial intelligence (AI), providing accurate and objective feedback.

Beyond learning and testing, the application also promotes effective communication with its real-time sign language recognition feature. Simply make signs in front of the camera, and the AI will quickly recognize and display the results on the screen.

In addition to its core functionalities, the application also provides essential utilities such as note-taking, password recovery, and account authentication, ensuring a smooth and secure user experience.
